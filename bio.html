Biography

Krishna Soni is a fourth year student at Tufts University pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Physics and a Minor in computer science. His past experience includes extensive research using a wide array of different skillsets. Currently, he is finishing up his Bachelor's degree, and working as a Undergraduate Engineer at Draper Laboratory specializing in research with inertial navigation systems. Upon graduation, he plans on pursuing an advanced graduate degree and also a career in Aero/Astrospace engineering.

Krishna is a member of Tufts Theta Chi, and as philanthropy chair, he is responsible for planning and executing large scale fundraisers for internationally reknowned charities such as Direct Relief International, Habitat for Humanity, and the Brain and Behavior Foundation. He is also a part of FAUJ Bhangra, an award-winning, competitive, Indian dance team consisting of students all around the Boston area.
